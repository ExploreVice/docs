# IPTV Setup for Smart TVs

This guide covers IPTV installation for **Samsung, LG, and Hisense Smart TVs** running Tizen OS, WebOS, and other smart TV operating systems.

## Before You Begin

<Warning>
**Important**: These instructions are for **non-Android Smart TVs**. If your TV has Google Play Store, please follow the [Android TV guide](/iptv/android) instead.
</Warning>

### How to Identify Your TV Type

1. **Android TV**: Look for Google Play Store app on your TV
2. **Tizen OS**: Samsung interface with Samsung Apps store
3. **WebOS**: LG interface with LG Content Store
4. **Hisense VIDAA**: Hisense interface with VIDAA store

## Installation Methods

### Method 1: IPTV Smarters Pro (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access App Store">
    - **Samsung**: Open Samsung Apps store
    - **LG**: Open LG Content Store
    - **Hisense**: Open VIDAA store
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for IPTV App">
    Search for "IPTV Smarters Pro" or similar IPTV applications available for your TV
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download and Install">
    Download and install the IPTV application on your Smart TV
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch and Configure">
    1. Open the installed IPTV app
    2. Select "Add New User" or "Login"
    3. Enter your IPTV subscription details:
       - **Name**: Choose any name
       - **Username**: Your IPTV username
       - **Password**: Your IPTV password
       - **URL**: Your IPTV server URL
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: M3U Playlist (Alternative)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Built-in Media Player">
    Some Smart TVs have built-in media players that support M3U playlists
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add M3U URL">
    1. Open your TV's media player or IPTV section
    2. Look for "Add URL" or "Network" options
    3. Enter your M3U playlist URL
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Load Channels">
    The TV will load your channel list from the M3U playlist
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App Not Available in Store">
    If IPTV Smarters Pro isn't available for your TV model:
    - Try searching for alternative IPTV apps like "Smart IPTV" or "SS IPTV"
    - Consider using the M3U playlist method
    - Contact our support for device-specific recommendations
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Channels Not Loading">
    - Check your internet connection
    - Verify your IPTV credentials are correct
    - Ensure your subscription is active
    - Try restarting the app or TV
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Poor Video Quality">
    - Check your internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps recommended)
    - Adjust video quality settings in the app
    - Try connecting via ethernet instead of Wi-Fi
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance Tips

<Tip>
**Optimize Your Smart TV Performance:**
- Close unused apps to free up memory
- Restart your TV regularly
- Keep your TV's firmware updated
- Use a wired internet connection when possible
</Tip>
