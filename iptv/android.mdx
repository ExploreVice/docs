## Before You Begin

<Info>
**Identifying Android TV**: Look for the Google Play Store app on your TV. If present, you have an Android TV and should follow this guide.
</Info>

### Requirements

- Android 5.0 (API level 21) or higher
- Stable internet connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
- Google Play Store access

## Installation Guide

### Step 1: Install IPTV Smarters Pro

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Google Play Store">
    Launch the Google Play Store on your Android device
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Search for IPTV App">
    Search for "IPTV Smarters Pro" in the search bar
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Install the App">
    1. Select "IPTV Smarters Pro" from search results
    2. Tap "Install" to download and install the app
    3. Wait for the installation to complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch the App">
    Open IPTV Smarters Pro from your app drawer or home screen
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Step 2: Configure Your IPTV Subscription

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add New User">
    1. Tap "Add New User" on the main screen
    2. Select "Login with Xtream Codes API"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Credentials">
    Fill in your IPTV subscription details:
    - **Name**: Choose any display name
    - **Username**: Your IPTV username
    - **Password**: Your IPTV password  
    - **URL**: Your IPTV server URL
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save and Connect">
    1. Tap "Add User" to save your configuration
    2. The app will connect to your IPTV service
    3. Wait for the channel list to load
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Device-Specific Instructions

### Android TV (Google TV)

<Accordion title="Android TV Setup">
  1. Use your TV remote to navigate to Google Play Store
  2. Search and install IPTV Smarters Pro
  3. Use the on-screen keyboard to enter credentials
  4. Navigate using your TV remote's directional pad
</Accordion>

### Android Phone/Tablet

<Accordion title="Mobile Device Setup">
  1. Open Google Play Store app
  2. Search and install IPTV Smarters Pro
  3. Use touch screen to enter credentials
  4. Enable landscape mode for better viewing experience
</Accordion>

### Android TV Box

<Accordion title="TV Box Setup">
  1. Connect your TV box to the internet
  2. Open Google Play Store (or sideload if needed)
  3. Install IPTV Smarters Pro
  4. Use the included remote or connect a wireless keyboard
</Accordion>

## Advanced Configuration

### M3U Playlist Method

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get M3U URL">
    Obtain your M3U playlist URL from your IPTV provider
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Playlist">
    1. In IPTV Smarters Pro, tap "Add New User"
    2. Select "Load Your Playlist or File/URL"
    3. Choose "M3U URL" option
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    - **Playlist Name**: Choose a name
    - **M3U URL**: Enter your playlist URL
    - **EPG URL**: (Optional) Enter EPG URL for program guide
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App Won't Install">
    - Ensure you have enough storage space
    - Check your Android version compatibility
    - Try clearing Google Play Store cache
    - Restart your device and try again
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Channels Not Loading">
    - Verify your internet connection
    - Check IPTV credentials are correct
    - Ensure your subscription is active
    - Try switching to a different server
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Buffering Issues">
    - Check your internet speed (minimum 10 Mbps)
    - Close other apps using bandwidth
    - Try lowering video quality in app settings
    - Switch to a wired connection if possible
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="App Crashes">
    - Clear app cache and data
    - Restart your device
    - Update the app to the latest version
    - Free up device storage space
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance Optimization

<Tip>
**Optimize Your Android IPTV Experience:**
- **Close background apps** to free up RAM
- **Use hardware acceleration** if available in app settings
- **Connect via ethernet** for stable connection on TV boxes
- **Adjust buffer size** in advanced settings
- **Enable hardware decoding** for better performance
</Tip>
