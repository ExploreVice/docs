# IPTV Setup for iOS & Apple TV

This guide covers IPTV installation for **iPhone, iPad, and Apple TV** devices running iOS and tvOS.

## Supported Apple Devices

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="iPhone" icon="mobile">
    iPhone 6s and later with iOS 12+
  </Card>
  <Card title="iPad" icon="tablet">
    iPad Air 2 and later with iOS 12+
  </Card>
  <Card title="Apple TV" icon="tv">
    Apple TV 4th gen and later with tvOS 12+
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Before You Begin

### System Requirements

- **iOS**: iOS 12.0 or later
- **tvOS**: tvOS 12.0 or later
- **Internet**: Stable Wi-Fi connection (minimum 10 Mbps)
- **Apple ID**: Required for App Store access

<Warning>
**Important**: Due to Apple's App Store policies, dedicated IPTV apps may have limited availability. Alternative methods may require additional steps.
</Warning>

## Installation Methods

### Method 1: App Store Applications

#### IPTV Apps Available on App Store

<Steps>
  <Step title="Search App Store">
    Look for these IPTV-compatible apps:
    - **GSE Smart IPTV** (Recommended)
    - **IPTV Player**
    - **VLC for Mobile**
    - **Infuse 7**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download and Install">
    1. Tap **Get** to download the app
    2. Authenticate with Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode
    3. Wait for installation to complete
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Launch and Configure">
    Open the installed app and follow the setup process
  </Step>
</Steps>

#### GSE Smart IPTV Setup (Recommended)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download GSE Smart IPTV">
    Install GSE Smart IPTV from the App Store
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Playlist">
    1. Open GSE Smart IPTV
    2. Tap the **+** button to add a new playlist
    3. Select **M3U URL** or **Xtream Codes**
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Configure Settings">
    **For M3U URL:**
    - **Name**: Choose a playlist name
    - **URL**: Enter your M3U playlist URL
    - **EPG URL**: (Optional) Enter EPG URL
    
    **For Xtream Codes:**
    - **Name**: Choose a display name
    - **Server**: Your IPTV server URL
    - **Username**: Your IPTV username
    - **Password**: Your IPTV password
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save and Load">
    1. Tap **Save** to add the playlist
    2. Wait for channels to load
    3. Start watching your content
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Method 2: VLC for Mobile

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install VLC">
    Download VLC for Mobile from the App Store
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Network Stream">
    1. Open VLC
    2. Tap **Network** tab
    3. Tap **+** to add a new stream
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter M3U URL">
    1. Enter your M3U playlist URL
    2. Tap **Save**
    3. Tap the saved stream to start playing
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Steps>

    Configure IPTV on your iPhone or iPad using GSE Smart IPTV
  </Step>

## Configuration Examples

### M3U Playlist Configuration

<Accordion title="M3U Setup Example">
**Example M3U URL format:**
```
http://your-provider.com/get.php?username=your_user&password=your_pass&type=m3u_plus
```

**In GSE Smart IPTV:**
1. Select "M3U URL" when adding playlist
2. Enter the complete URL
3. Optionally add EPG URL for program guide
4. Save and let the app load channels
</Accordion>

### Xtream Codes Configuration

<Accordion title="Xtream Codes Setup Example">
**Required Information:**
- **Server URL**: `http://your-provider.com:port`
- **Username**: Your account username
- **Password**: Your account password

**In GSE Smart IPTV:**
1. Select "Xtream Codes" when adding playlist
2. Enter server URL (without username/password in URL)
3. Enter username and password separately
4. Save configuration
</Accordion>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="App Not Available in App Store">
    **Solutions:**
    - Try alternative apps like VLC or Infuse 7
    - Use web browser method on iPad
    - Consider AirPlay from another device
    - Check if your region restricts certain apps
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Playback Issues">
    **Common fixes:**
    - Check internet connection speed
    - Restart the app and device
    - Verify IPTV credentials
    - Try different server/quality settings
    - Clear app cache if available
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Apple TV Connection Issues">
    **Troubleshooting:**
    - Ensure Apple TV and iOS device are on same network
    - Restart both devices
    - Check AirPlay settings
    - Update to latest iOS/tvOS versions
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Buffering Problems">
    **Solutions:**
    - Check Wi-Fi signal strength
    - Close other bandwidth-heavy apps
    - Lower video quality in app settings
    - Restart your router
    - Try different server if available
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Performance Optimization

### iOS Devices (iPhone/iPad)

<Steps>
  <Step title="Optimize Device Performance">
    - Close unnecessary background apps
    - Ensure sufficient storage space
    - Keep iOS updated to latest version
    - Restart device regularly
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Network Optimization">
    - Use 5GHz Wi-Fi when available
    - Position device close to router
    - Avoid network congestion times
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Apple TV

<Steps>
  <Step title="Apple TV Optimization">
    - Keep tvOS updated
    - Restart Apple TV regularly
    - Check available storage
    - Use ethernet connection when possible
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Display Settings">
    - Adjust video resolution if needed
    - Enable/disable HDR based on content
    - Optimize for your TV's capabilities
  </Step>
</Steps>
