## Redeeming the Key

<Steps>
    <Step title="First Step">
        After purchasing and receiving the key, go to the [Astra.rip Login Website](https://astra.rip/account/login), press Sign up and create an account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Second Step">
        Now, login to your created account paste the license key into the field to redeem it and bind it to your account
    </Step>
</Steps>

    </Step>
